Dogs

Owning a dog is great fun and immensely rewarding. But, dogs have complex needs and each dog is unique. There is no 'perfect' way to care for a dog, but owner mission is to make dog healthy and happy.
Some facts about dogs
1) The fastest recorded speed for a greyhound is 42 miles per hour, similar to that of a mounted racehorse, which can reach speeds of around 43 miles per hour!
2) Dogs have a sense of time. It's been proven that they know the difference between a hour and five. If conditioned to, they can predict future events, such as regular walk times.
3) The United States has the highest pet dog population in the world. Approximately 75.8 million in fact.
